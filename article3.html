Jam Session Gospel

Jam Crew Mallorca | James Davies

“We got a little attitude from Roy Acuff,” Hanna says. “But that was him just looking at us and judging the book by its cover. He loosened up immediately as soon as we started playing together.

By the time the project was completed, a wondrous collection of country pioneers were on tape with the Dirt Band: Scruggs, Johnny Cash, Martin, Acuff, Watson, Vassar Clements, Junior Husky, Oswald Kirby, even Mother Maybelle Carter.

I ask Hanna what it was like to play with Mother Maybelle Carter. “Oh, man,” he replies, then pauses, as if to gather his thoughts. Forty-five years later, it remains a big deal for him. He tells me how Scruggs helped bring Carter into the Dirt Band's fold. When Scruggs confirmed Carter's participation, Hanna says, “I'm immediately thinking about my Pete Seeger instructional book for playing the guitar. And in that instruction book was how to play ‘Wildwood Flower.’”
